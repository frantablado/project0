Renault Clio Mio 2013
This was my first “0 km” as we call it in Argentina, what it means my first car fresh out of the assembly line. I loved this car, it was above all, a simple car. It was in many ways better than the Atos, but it did have me some troubles. Also it was not a safe car, specially for the road. Still, it took me to many far places with certain comfort.
Security It’s biggest weakness.
By that time, it was consider an insecure car. Had ABS breaks, and that’s it. No airbag, no stability control. With all that it was declare by the LatinCap as one of the most insecure cars made in Latin America (it was mae here in Argentina). It was also a car easy to rob, that is why I shaded the windows security film.
Maintenance Easy and cheap
It was cheap to maintain, very cheap, even doing the service in and official Service Center, for witch I went every time. I got it for over 60.000 km and the most expensive part I have to buy was the battery, that was around 35 usd. In terms of gas consumption: wasn’t bad, but not great.
Reliability It was OK.
I have never had a problem with the engine, that was preaty reliable, witch is good. But I did had problems with several of other parts like the exaust (it just felt off in the middle of the road one day), interior parts, the lock of the trunk, the AC and some lights of the console stop working.
Agility Great weight/power ratio
It was a very ligh car with a 1.2 litters engine. That made of it a very agile car in the city. That was it strong suit for sure. It was fun to drive.
Comfort It has the basics
AC, electric windows, and a audio system came with aux jack and Bluetooth (a huge plus for me). The seats were OK but not ideal for long trips. Also it was very loud in the interior. The sound proof investment on this car was very poor. 4 people could travel with reasonable comfort. Did not have power steering
Practicality Less is more
The trunk space in this case was quite good. Also could put down the back seats and get room for moving some large staff. Some how, the Atos was some how roomier in this point. As simple as it was, that made it simple to use and maintain.
Pros
  • Agility in traffic
  • Great cost/benefit
  • Cheap and simple to maintain
Cons
  • Lack of security
  • No much comfort
